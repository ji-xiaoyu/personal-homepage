Master of Communication

Xiaoyu Ji

I am Xiaoyu Ji, a master student advised by Prof. Xiaokun Wu of the School of Journalism and Communication at South China University of Technology(SCUT). My research interests include Online Public Opinion, Data News, New Media and Social Governance. Now, I am also a member of the Public Opinion Research Center in SCUT. My thesis theme is about "Hate Speech and Social Conflict "as a sub-research supported by the Key Project of the National Social Science Fund, which is led by my supervisor. Typically, I would obtain my Master's Degree in Communication in June 2021.
